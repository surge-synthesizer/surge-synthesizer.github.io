Since Surge XT 1.3.0, we support Open Sound Control protocol. This enables remote control of various aspects of Surge XT via network connection.
One such popular tool for remote control is [TouchOSC](https://hexler.net/touchosc).

Feel free to check out this TouchOSC template on your tablet or any other device capable of running TouchOSC. The latest version requires Surge 1.3.2.

</a>
